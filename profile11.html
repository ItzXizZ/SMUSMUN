Aidan Mackay | Director | Aidan.mackay@smus.ca


An 11th grader currently attending SMUS, Aidan is eager to serve as the Director of the Historical Crisis Committee at SMUSMUN 2023. An avid participant in MUN, Aidan began his MUN journey in grade 6 in the UNESCO committee as Ukraine. After speaking only twice during his first conference, Aidan is now an enthusiastic enjoyer of crisis committees, after participating in his first in grade 8. Some of his favourite experiences have been getting publicly executed by lightsaber after being a spy for over half of a conference, going to Whistler with his friends for VYMUN 2019, and winning Best Delegate for his role as Bismarck in the Franco-Prussian war. Above all, Aidan enjoys the variety that comes with MUN, and all of the connections that you can form at conferences. Outside of committee rooms, Aidan procrastinates his heaps of AP homework, plays video games with his friends, and goes to the gym. MUN has been a big part of Aidan’s life, and he hopes to share his passion for it with this year’s attendants of SMUSMUN.
