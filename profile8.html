Zephyr Fisher-Frank | Director | Zephyr.fisher-franke@smus.ca


Upon joining Model UN at 12, Zephyr realised that she could put her debate skills to something other than arguing with her sister. Since then she has participated in a variety of committees, ranging from the US Senate, to the Franco-Prussian War, and served as Chair for HRC during the first SMUS MUN. Apart from Model UN, Zephyr enjoys mock trials, organising weekly Politics Club meetings, and pretends to enjoy going for runs around the golf course near her house. In her free time she enjoys editing the student paper, reading books, or can be found holed up in her room re-watching episodes of Suits. Zephyr’s claim to fame is having a drink named after her at SMUS’s cafe because of how much coffee she drinks, and she can usually be found trying to tell anyone who will listen about her AP Research project. Zephyr is incredibly thrilled to be the Director for OPEC and promises exciting crises, great debates, and lots of fun for all degates!
