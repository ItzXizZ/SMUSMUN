Daniel Benjamin | Chair


Navigating his final year of high school at St. Michaels University School, Daniel is honoured to be the Crisis Committee Chair. Upon entering his first conference five years ago, he didn’t realise the impact MUN would have on his life. He thinks that Model UN is far bigger than a debate/public speaking competition; it provides a space for students to grow in confidence, knowledge, and inspire a lifetime of political interest and efficacy. Some of Daniel’s best MUN experiences include his first midnight crisis at VYMUN to making countless friends and memories throughout the years. When not passionately debating, Daniel can be found studying, watching Jeopardy, or reading for hours on end. His other interests include history, philosophy, and aerospace. Daniel hopes that his last SMUSMUN will be a conference that creates new memories for him and others, and inspires delegates to continue in MUN.
